About Me

Ten years ago, I never thought I would be trying to pursue a career within web technologies. I was adamant in pursuing a career in political journalism, until I reached a crossroads moment questioning myself if this is what I want for the rest of my life. I doubted my soon to be profession because I simply became intrigued in the "wonders" of the web world. At the time everything was a mystery to me, and I would ask myself, "How cool would it be if I knew how to design or actually learn how to code a website or an app?" Then one day I stopped wondering and decided to give my curiosity a chance - and it was the best risk I took that I will never regret.
